Admission Process

Step-by-step guide to apply to MHCS Seminary

1

Preparation & Discernment

Take time to pray and discern your vocation. Consult with your spiritual director, family, and parish priest about your interest in seminary formation.

2

Obtain Application Materials

Request an application packet from the Admissions Office or download it from our website. Review all requirements and gather necessary documents.

3

Complete Application

Submit your completed application form, essay, and required documents by the deadline. Ensure all sections are filled out completely and accurately.

4

Interview & Assessment

Qualified applicants will be invited for interviews, psychological testing, and medical examination. These assessments help us understand your readiness for seminary life.

5

Formation Team Review

The Admissions and Formation team reviews all materials and assessments to determine your candidacy for seminary formation.

6

Final Decision & Enrollment

Accepted candidates receive an admission letter and further instructions for enrollment. We will guide you through the registration and orientation process.
