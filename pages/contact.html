Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

When is the best time to contact the alumni office?

The alumni office is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. We respond to emails within 24 hours during business days.

How do I update my directory information?

You can update your information directly on our website through the Alumni Directory page, or contact the alumni office via email at alumni@mhcsalumni.org with the changes you'd like to make.

Can I arrange a campus visit?

Yes! Alumni are always welcome to visit the campus. Please contact the main office at least two weeks in advance to arrange a convenient visit time. Tours are typically available during business hours.

Who should I contact about making a donation?

For donation inquiries, please reach out to our Development Office at donations@mhcsalumni.org or call +63 2 8888 9012. We'll be happy to discuss how you can support our various projects.

How can I volunteer for alumni events?

We love volunteers! Please contact the alumni office at alumni@mhcsalumni.org or call +63 2 8888 5678 to express your interest. We have opportunities throughout the year for various events and initiatives.
