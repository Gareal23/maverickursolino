Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our reunions and events

How do I register for a reunion? +

You can register online using the form below or contact your batch representative directly. Online registration is the fastest way to secure your spot.

What is included in the registration fee? +

The registration fee typically includes dinner, beverages, and program materials. Some events may include accommodation. Check the specific event details for what's included.

Can I bring a plus one? +

Yes! You can bring a guest. Please indicate this in your registration form. Additional fee applies for plus-ones (typically 50% of the registration fee).

What if I have special dietary requirements? +

Please indicate your dietary requirements during registration. We will do our best to accommodate vegetarian, vegan, and other dietary restrictions.

Is there a refund policy? +

Cancellations made 30 days or more before the event are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 30 days are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person.
